About Us

"Our mission is to provide a thorough, detailed and affordable home inspection to help you make a confident, informed decision regarding your new home purchase. We meticulously inspect all accessible components for signs of damage, disrepair, or possible safety hazards.

Anything out of the ordinary is photographed and documented in a comprehensive inspection report. And, our work doesn’t stop there; we’re always available to address any questions or concerns about our findings.
